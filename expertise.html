Published Research & Contributions

My Expertise

Biomechanics & Prosthesis Design

Key Works: Gait Cycle Analysis, Pelvic Reconstruction

My doctoral and post-doctoral research extensively explored the Topology Optimization of the human hip and pelvic bones. I have developed computational models to simulate various activities of daily living, including walking, running, and single-leg stance.

A significant portion of my work focuses on the Design of Pelvic Prostheses using multi-load approach topology optimization. By analyzing the complex loading patterns of the gait cycle, I have proposed optimal material distributions that minimize compliance and reduce stress shielding, paving the way for more durable and patient-specific orthopedic implants.

Quantum-Inspired Optimization

Key Works: Magnetic Lattices, Hybrid Algorithms

At BosonQ Psi, I am pioneering the application of Quantum-Inspired Evolutionary Optimization Algorithms (QIEOA). My work involves benchmarking these algorithms on high-performance GPUs to solve large-scale engineering problems that challenge classical solvers.

Recent contributions include the Design of Magnetic Lattices and the development of Hybrid Algorithms that combine quantum-inspired techniques with gradient-based methods. These innovations are critical for exploring vast design spaces in structural topology optimization with greater efficiency and speed.

Computational Mechanics & AI

Key Works: CNNs, SBFEM, Topological Derivatives

I advocate for the integration of modern computational techniques into structural design. My research includes using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) to accelerate topology optimization processes, effectively bridging Machine Learning with solid mechanics.

Additionally, I have worked on Shape Optimization of Beams using the Scaled Boundary Finite Element Method (SBFEM) and B-Splines, as well as implementing Topological Derivatives as an evolutionary approach. This diverse toolkit allows me to tackle complex design constraints, such as specified fundamental natural frequencies and vibration controls.
