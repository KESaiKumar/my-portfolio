About Me

Innovating at the Intersection of Mechanics & Quantum Computing

I am currently serving as a Principal Optimization Scientist at BosonQ Psi. My work focuses on harnessing the power of quantum computing to solve complex engineering optimization problems that are intractable for classical systems.

Prior to my current role, I was a Research Associate at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore, and an Institute Post-Doctoral Fellow at IIT Madras. I earned my PhD from IIT Madras, where my thesis focused on the topology optimization of the hip bone structure.
